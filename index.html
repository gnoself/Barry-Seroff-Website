Barry Seroff

For over 20 years, Barry Seroff has been performing classical and jazz on guitar and flute in a variety of settings. From renaissance music to American standards and everything in between, he can bring a touch of class to any festive gathering. Looking for something more exotic? Barry can present the zen flute sounds of the shakuhachi. Perhaps something European is more to your taste; Barry can bring the music straight to your guests as a ‘wandering musician’. He can even compose music specially for the occasion. From specific musical requests to general ambiance, leave it to Barry to create an elegant musical experience.
